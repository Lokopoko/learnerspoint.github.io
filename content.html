3. Russia’s COVID-19 vaccine Sputnik V will be produced in India by generic pharmaceutical firm Hetero.

4. Ministry SJ&E launched a national portal for transgender persons to apply for certificates and identity cards. The portal had been developed within two months of the Ministry notifying the Transgender Persons (Protection of Rights) Rules, 2020, on September 29.

5. The Marine Commandos (MARCOS) of the Indian Navy have been deployed in the Pangong lake area in eastern Ladakh. The idea behind the deployment of the MARCOS in Eastern Ladakh where Indian Air Force’s Garud operatives and Indian Army’s Para Special Forces, which have been there since day one of the conflicts, is to enhance the integration of the three services.

6. Former Orissa High Court Judge Justice Bijoy Krishna Patel has been appointed as the chairperson of Odisha Human Rights Commission.

7. The Prayagraj Express became the fastest train with 24 coaches operated by the Indian Railways, clocking a speed of 130 kmph.

8. FIFA released its final rankings list for 2020 where Belgium bagged No. 1 position. Defending World Cup champion France is in second place, with Brazil third and England fourth. FIFA will host the European draw on December 7.

9. The QS Asia University rankings 2021 have been released and a total of 107 Indian institutes have been selected among the total 650 universities ranked.

10. The Indira Gandhi International (IGI) Airport has become Asia Pacific’s first Level 4+ (Transition) accredited airport under ACI’s Airport Carbon Accreditation (ACA) programme. ACA is the global standard for carbon management in the airport industry.
